To the woman who built my world

I don’t think I ever found the right words to begin this, because nothing I say will ever translate the weight of what you’ve carried.

But I made this anyway—because some feelings don’t ask to be perfect. They just ask to be seen.

“If love had a shape, it would look like the way you care—quietly, endlessly, and even on moments when no one noticed.”
